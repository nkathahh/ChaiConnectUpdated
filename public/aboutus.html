Our Story

ChaiConnect was born from a simple vision: to bridge the gap between farmers, factories, extension officers and administrators in the tea industry. Our platform provides a transparent, efficient, and digital solution to tea farming operations, ensuring better communication, fairer pricing and streamlined workflows across the supply chain.

Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize the tea farming ecosystem by offering a smart management system that supports every stakeholder‚Äî from smallholder farmers to factory staff and policymakers. Our goal is to increase productivity, traceability and trust within the industry.

What We Offer

Through ChaiConnect, users can:

  • Track and manage tea deliveries
  • Monitor quality and payment rates
  • Communicate directly with support officers
  • Access training, policies, and system alerts
  • Generate reports and insights for better decision-making
