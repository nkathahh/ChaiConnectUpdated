How It Works

One Platform. Every Stakeholder.

ChaiConnect gives each role in the tea supply chain a tailored, focused experience.

01
🌿

Tea Farmers

Register once and get a unique farmer ID. Log daily green leaf deliveries, view your running payment balance, track quality grades per delivery

  • Daily delivery logging
  • Payment history & statements
  • Quality grade feedback
  • Training schedules & notices
02
🏭

Factory Staff

Receive and verify incoming green leaf at the weighbridge. Assign quality grades, generate farmer payment summaries, and push automated notifications back to farmers in real time.

  • Weighbridge intake recording
  • Grade assignment (A – C)
  • Automated payment computation
  • Bulk farmer notification
03
👨‍🌾

Extension Officers

Plan field visits, log agronomic observations, share training materials and best-practice bulletins directly to the farmers in your zone. Track attendance and generate visit reports digitally.

  • Visit scheduling & logging
  • Farmer progress tracking
  • Training material distribution
  • Zone performance reports
04
🛡️

Administrators

Manage user accounts, approve new registrations, configure payment rates and quality thresholds, and oversee system-wide analytics and audit logs across all factories and zones.

  • User management & approvals
  • Payment rate configuration
  • System-wide audit logs
  • Cross-factory dashboards
Platform Features

Built for Kenya's Tea Reality

Every feature was designed around the actual challenges faced by farmers, factories and officers on the ground.

📦

Delivery Tracking

Every kilogram of green leaf is recorded at intake — date, weight, grade and farmer ID. Full traceability from farm to processing, with a permanent audit trail.

💰

Transparent Payments

Farmers see exactly how their payment is calculated — weight × grade rate — before settlement. No more guesswork or disputes at end-of-month.

🔐

Role-Based Access

Farmers, factory staff, extension officers and admins each see only what is relevant to them. Sensitive financial data is strictly compartmentalised.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Generate delivery summaries, quality trend reports and payment registers — weekly, monthly or for custom date ranges — and export to PDF or Excel.

📱

USSD Access

Dial *384# from any basic phone to check your latest delivery, view your balance or receive quality feedback. No internet or smartphone needed.

🔔

SMS Notifications

Automatic SMS alerts when a delivery is logged, when payment is processed, or when an extension officer posts a new bulletin to your zone.
