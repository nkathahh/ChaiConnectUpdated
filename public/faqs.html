Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ChaiConnect — answered clearly and simply. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly.

General

What is ChaiConnect? ▼
ChaiConnect is a digital management platform for Kenya's tea supply chain. It connects smallholder farmers, tea factory staff, agricultural extension officers and system administrators in one shared system — enabling real-time delivery logging, transparent payment calculation, quality grading, and field extension services.
Is ChaiConnect free to use? ▼
Registration and access for individual farmers is completely free. Factory and cooperative accounts are set up by system administrators. If you are a factory manager or cooperative administrator looking to onboard your organisation, please contact us to discuss access and any applicable setup process.
Does ChaiConnect work offline or without a smartphone? ▼
Yes. Farmers can access key information via USSD by dialling *384# from any mobile phone — no internet connection or smartphone required. Core features such as delivery balance checks, latest grade, and payment status are all available through USSD. SMS notifications are also sent automatically for key events.
Which counties and cooperatives does ChaiConnect support? ▼
ChaiConnect is currently used by cooperatives across Nyeri, Kericho, Kisii, Meru, Nandi, Embu and Kirinyaga counties. We are actively onboarding new cooperatives. If your cooperative is not yet on the platform, please reach out through the Contact Us page and we will initiate the onboarding process.

For Farmers

How do I register as a farmer? ▼
Click the Register button on the top navigation. Fill in your full name, national ID number, phone number, email address, location and a profile photo. Once submitted, your details are reviewed by your cooperative's administrator. You will receive an SMS when your account is approved, after which you can log in.
How do I check my tea delivery history? ▼
Once logged in, your farmer dashboard shows a full list of all deliveries recorded under your farmer ID — including date, weight (kg), quality grade assigned, applicable rate, and the calculated payment amount for each delivery. You can also request a printable statement for any date range.
How is my payment amount calculated? ▼
Payment is calculated as: Weight (kg) × Grade Rate (KES/kg). Grade rates are set by the cooperative administrator and are visible in your dashboard. Grade A receives the highest rate, followed by B, C and D. Any applicable deductions (e.g. transport levies) are shown as separate line items on your statement so you can see exactly how your net payment is arrived at.
What do the quality grades mean? ▼
Quality grades reflect the condition of the green leaf delivered:

Grade A — Two leaves and a bud, clean, no bruising. Highest rate.
Grade B — Mostly two-and-a-bud, minor imperfections.
Grade C — Mixed leaf, some coarse material present.
Grade D — Coarse, bruised or damaged leaf. Lowest rate.

Extension officers can advise on best practices for harvesting Grade A leaf consistently.
What if I believe my delivery weight was recorded incorrectly? ▼
If you believe there is an error in a recorded delivery, you should first speak to the factory intake supervisor and reference your delivery slip number. If the issue cannot be resolved at the factory level, contact ChaiConnect support via the Contact Us page or speak to your extension officer, who can raise a formal dispute on your behalf.
How do I use the USSD service? ▼
Dial *384# from the phone number registered to your account. You will be presented with a menu to check your latest delivery, view your running balance, see your most recent quality grade, or hear about upcoming extension training events in your zone. No internet is required — this works on any basic mobile phone.

For Factory Staff

How do factory staff accounts get created? ▼
Factory staff accounts are created and managed by a system administrator. Individual staff members do not self-register — your factory's ChaiConnect administrator will set up your account, assign your role (e.g. intake clerk, factory manager) and provide you with login credentials. Contact your supervisor or the ChaiConnect support team if you need an account set up.
How do I record an incoming delivery from a farmer? ▼
Log in to your staff dashboard and navigate to Intake → New Delivery. Enter the farmer's ID number (or scan if your station has a barcode reader), enter the weight from the weighbridge, assign the quality grade, and confirm. The system will immediately record the delivery, calculate the estimated payment, and trigger an SMS notification to the farmer.
Can I edit a delivery entry after it has been submitted? ▼
Entry corrections can be made within two hours of submission by the recording clerk. After this window, corrections require approval from a factory manager. All edits are logged in the system audit trail — original values and corrected values are both retained for accountability.
How do I generate an end-of-month payment report? ▼
Navigate to Reports → Payment Summary. Select the factory, the month and year, and click Generate. The report will display total deliveries, total weight per farmer, grade breakdown and computed gross payment. You can export the report as a PDF or Excel file for submission to your cooperative's accounts department.

For Extension Officers

How do I log a farm visit? ▼
In your officer dashboard, go to Visits → Log New Visit. Select the farmer from your zone, enter the visit date, purpose (agronomic advisory, pest & disease, soil management, etc.), your observations, and any follow-up actions recommended. The farmer's record is updated immediately and the visit is included in your zone report.
How do I share training materials or bulletins with farmers? ▼
Navigate to Bulletins → New Bulletin. Write your title and content (or attach a PDF document), select the target zone or individual farmers, and publish. Farmers with smartphones will see the bulletin in their dashboard. Farmers without internet access will receive an SMS summary with a USSD shortcode to hear key points.
Can I see the delivery and quality trends for farmers in my zone? ▼
Yes. Your dashboard includes a Zone Overview showing delivery volumes and average quality grades for all farmers assigned to your zone. This helps you identify which farmers may benefit from a focused agronomic visit — for example, farmers whose Grade A percentage has declined over recent weeks.

Account & Security

I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ▼
On the login page, click Forgot Password. Enter the email address or ID number associated with your account. A password reset link will be sent to your registered email. If you no longer have access to that email, contact ChaiConnect support with your national ID number for identity verification and manual reset.
How is my personal and financial data protected? ▼
All data is stored in an encrypted database. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Access to data is strictly controlled by your assigned role — a farmer can only see their own records, factory staff can only see records for their factory, and so on. The platform uses HTTPS for all data in transit. Audit logs are maintained for all sensitive operations.
Can I update my phone number or email address? ▼
Yes. Log in and go to My Profile → Edit Details. You can update your phone number, email and location. Note that changes to your national ID number or full name require administrator approval to prevent fraudulent alterations, and you may be asked to provide supporting documentation.
How do I report a problem or give feedback? ▼
Use the Contact Us page to reach our support team directly. Include as much detail as possible — your farmer ID or account email, the feature you were using, and a description of the issue. For urgent payment disputes, also speak to your cooperative's administrator or extension officer who can escalate on your behalf.

Still have a question?
